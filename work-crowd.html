Crowd Delivery

Back

As an intern at Crowd Delivery I was responsible for updating and designing the blog as well as other multimedia content within the company.

Alongside managing the company's Wordpress blog and styling the site, I also photographed, videographed, and created multimedia content for them.

The problem

Crowd Delivery wanted to reach out to more people but in a personalised manner.

They wanted the content they provided to their clients to feel unique and customised to them.

The solution

We decided to create a website that had blog articles published every other day. The content had puns and humour in order to make users feel welcomed to the content.

We also made sure that the content is related to them, we wrote articles that were related to the seasons, and the occasions.
