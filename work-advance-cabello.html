Advance Cabello is a company in Thailad that distributes high quality hair products nation-wide.

I had the opportunity to create a website for our client that represented the company. The company is based in Bangkok, Thailand, as the country is not billingual it was important that the website is translatable to Thai.

The problem

Our client wanted a website to not only represent their company, but displays the products they produce and allow users to understand the company's goals.

It was important that the website was mobile friendly and also translated to Thai promptly as not all Advance Cabello's clients read English.

The solution

After a few prototypes, I came up with the final solution and started implementing the webiste.

I wrote the website in HTML and styled in SCSS. I made sure that the website was mobile friendly.

By using JavaScript, I was able to make the website translate to Thai promptly and immediately.
