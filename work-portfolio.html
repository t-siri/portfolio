This project was not necessarily a client project but I wanted to showcase this work.

My first portfolio I have designed, built and deployed. I wanted to build this to have an intial portfolio people can view.

The problem

I had to find a way to build a portfolio that represented me at the time but also showed my skillsets.

As technology and design changes everyday, my first portfolio eventually became out of date.

The solution

I have decided to keep my old portfolio as an old project I could have a look at and reference off while I created a new portfolio (this current one).

It was clear the old portfolio was outdated, but as design has patterns of repetition, I didn't want to destroy my work.
